The default head is a REALISTIC human bust shape (owner-ratified 2026-07-13), with the
text skin as its entire visible surface. Surface realism (albedo, pores) is explicitly
irrelevant; shape, topology, UVs, and morphs are what the asset buys.

Source: primary is ICT-FaceKit Light (MIT, verified from the repository licence), the
only surveyed candidate bundling a real quad human head, documented UVs, and 53 ARKit
expression blendshapes as in-repo data whose licence survives sublicensing inside an npm
package. Backup: MPFB2 using only its bundled CC0 data (base mesh plus 34 FACS expression
units). This supersedes res.asset-sourcing's MakeHuman-first ranking after hands-on
verification showed MPFB2 does not bundle the claimed ARKit or viseme target data (name
lists only, resolved from a separately installed pack with a licence to re-verify).
Disqualified on verified licence grounds: FLAME, Basel Face Model, MetaHuman, 3D Scan
Store. Neither surviving route ships native visemes: the 15 canonical viseme morphs are
authored by compositing expression deltas in the committed asset pipeline, with Blender
sculpting as fallback only; validateRig asserts all 27 canonical targets.

Speech fixtures: the mode-2 e2e path uses a strictly Polly-shaped fixture generated by a
committed dev-only espeak-ng script (GPLv3 tool kept outside the shipped package; its
timeline output treated as data), plus a canonical VisemeFrame timeline fixture covering
all 15 morphs because Polly's alphabet cannot express viseme_nn. Kokoro (Apache-2.0) is
the recorded future candidate for a local in-browser mode-2 adapter and stays deferred
(dec.speech-architecture); the AudioEngine PCM connection seam it needs is recorded in
res.seam-audit and is not part of v2.
